  
**Scene 1: Encounter NPC (Introduce Fire Risk Concepts)**

* Visual:  
  * A traveler sitting near a small, controlled fire  
  * His fire is placed in a clear, open area  
* Dialogue:  
  * “You’re not from around here, are you?”  
  * “If you’re staying the night, don’t rush your fire. Most people get that part wrong.”  
* Player choices:  
  1. “It’s just a fire. How hard can it be?”  
     * “Fire isn’t the danger… where you put it is.”  
  2. “What should I be careful about?”  
     * “Wind, ground, what’s around you… that decides everything.”  
* Dialogue:  
  * (player) “Also… I like to ask, do you know where to find \[Herb Name\]?”  
  * (NPC) “Ah… \[Herb Name\]? They grow around here, but you’ll want to watch for them in the dark.” “They have a way of standing out under the light of a fire.”  
* Animation:  
  * NPC gestures to surroundings (trees, wind, ground)  
  * Fire sparks briefly to visually hint danger  
  * Player walk away

**Scene 2:** 

* Visual (4 possible locations):  
  * Under low branches  
  * Near dry grass  
  * Close to player’s resting area (backpack visible)  
  * Open, clear ground  
* Dialogue:  
  * “If I’m building a fire here…”  
  * “…I need to think about where it will spread, not just where it starts.”  
*  Interaction (Click to inspect):  
  * Under low branches  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…There’s cover here…” *(pause)*  
      * “…No. If the flames rise—those branches will catch.”  
  * Near dry grass  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…This spot looks easy to light… But this grass would burn too fast.”  
  * Close to player’s resting area (backpack visible)  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…It’s close to my gear…That might not be a good idea.”  
  * Open, clear ground  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…This area is more open…Fewer things to catch fire nearby.  
      * “…It’s a good spot—but it’s not ready yet.”

**Scene 3:** 

* Visual:  
  * Selected fire location in last stage  
  * Ground still contains: Dry leaves Grass patches Small twigs  
* Dialogue:  
  * “I have to clean it first.” “Even a small flame could spread if I leave it like this.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Click/drag to clear leaves, grass, debris  
    * Feedback when dragging: “Too dry… this would catch immediately.”  
  * Drag rocks to form a fire ring  
    * Feedback when dragging: “This should keep it contained…”  
  * Click nearby objects (bushes/branches) to inspect surroundings  
    * Feedback for clicking bushes: “…Close enough to catch if the fire grows.”  
    * Feedback for clicking branches: “If the flames get higher… that could be a problem.”  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…That should do it.”  
  * “Now it won’t spread beyond where I intend.”

**Scene 4:** 

* Visual:  
  * Prepared fire site from Scene 4 (clean ground)  
  * Scattered materials visible:  
    * Dry leaves, bark, moss (tinder candidates)  
    * Small sticks (kindling)  
    * Large logs/branches (fuel wood)  
    * Some wet/dark materials near shaded or damp areas  
  * Player backpack icon  
* Dialogue:  
  * “A fire won’t build itself… I have to start building before the sun fully’s come down”  
* Interaction (Click to collect materials & Drag into backpack):  
  * Dry leaves, bark, moss (tinder candidates)  
    * Feedback: “Light, dry… this should catch quickly.”  
  * Small sticks (kindling)  
    * Feedback: “These will help the flame grow.”  
  * Large logs/branches (fuel wood)  
    * Feedback: “Too big to start with… but I’ll need them later.”  
  * Some wet/dark materials near shaded or damp areas  
    * Feedback: “…Too damp.” “This will smoke more than it burns.”

**Scene 4-1: Sorting Materials**

* Visual:  
  * Backpack opens  
  * Mixed pile of collected items  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…If I don’t organize this now…”  
  * “…I’ll make mistakes when it matters.”  
* Interaction (Drag & Drop Sorting):  
  * Tinder (smallest, easiest to ignite)  
  * Kindling (medium, supports growth)  
  * Fuel (largest, sustains fire)  
    * Feedback: “…Too damp.” “This will smoke more than it burns.”

**Scene 5: Constructing the Fire**

* Visual:  
  * Cleaned fire site with fire ring  
  * Materials laid out nearby, sorted into:  
    * Tinder (dry leaves, bark, moss)  
    * Kindling (small sticks)  
    * Fuel wood (medium to large logs)  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…All the materials are ready.”  
  * “…If I pile them randomly, it might not catch.”  
  * “…I need to structure this right, from small to large, so the fire grows steadily.”  
* Interaction (Drag & Drop / Click to Place Materials):  
  * Correct placement → first layer highlighted softly   
  * If player skips → later ignition may fail

**Scene 6: Start and Maintain the Fire**

* Visual:  
  * Fire site from Scene 5 with constructed fire (tinder \+ kindling stacked properly)  
  * Backpack icon on the corner  
  * Backpack inventory opens when clicked, showing:  
    * Flint & steel  
    * Matches (optional)  
    * Diary  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…The fire pit is ready, but nothing will burn until I spark the tinder.”  
  * “…Time to use the flint and steel.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player clicks backpack → selects flint & steel  
  * Player clicks/holds flint & steel over tinder to generate sparks  
    * Visual Feedback:  
      * Sparks land on tinder → small ember forms  
      * Smoke rises from ember → flicker appears  
    * Incorrect Actions:  
      * Strike too far from tinder → sparks miss → no ember  
      * Tinder packed too tightly → sparks smother → weak smoke  
* Dialogue:  
  * “Now it needs a little air… helps it grow without smothering the spark.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player clicks a “blow” button to simulate airflow  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…Now the flame’s alive, but it’s fragile.”  
  * “…I need to feed it small sticks first—slowly, not all at once.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player drags kindling into the flame gradually  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…It’s strong enough now.”  
  * “…Time to add larger wood—but carefully. If I rush this, I’ll kill the flame.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player places larger logs around burning kindling  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…Finally… a fire that will hold.”  
  * “…Now I can focus on what comes next.”

**Scene 7: Find Herbs (with fire on stick)**

* Visual:  
  * Player holding a small stick with flame from the fire  
  * Player standing in front of a bush  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…This flame should help me see in the dark.”  
  * “…The herbs… they’re supposed to glow under firelight.”  
  * “…Careful… I can’t let the flame touch the bushes, or it’ll spread.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player catch the fire with a stick in hand  
  * Player drags or moves the stick near glowing herbs
