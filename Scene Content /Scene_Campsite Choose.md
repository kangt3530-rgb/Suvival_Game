**Scene 1: Entering the Forest**

* Visual:  
  * Light gradually dims (late afternoon → evening)  
  * Wind blowing the tree  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…It’s farther than I thought. And the light’s fading already…”  
  * “If I keep going like this, I won’t make it through the night.”  
  * (pause, look around) “I need to stop here… just for now.”  
* Interaction (Inspect place by clicking visual):  
  * Uneven slope (rocks, tilted ground)    
    * Visual Feedback:  
      * Loose rocks shift underfoot  
      * Backpack slides slightly downhill  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…Too steep.”  
  * Near water (riverbank, damp soil, mist)   
    * Visual Feedback:  
      * Ground appears darker, slightly muddy  
      * Footstep leaves a wet imprint  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “The ground’s damp…”  
  * Near tree  
    * Visual Feedback:  
      * Leaf blown by wind  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…Dense cover there.”  
  * Flat, open ground (clear, stable area)   
    * Visual Feedback:  
      * Grass is short, ground even  
    * Dialogue:  
      * “…Clear… but exposed.”

**Scene 2: Encounter NPC**

* Visual:  
  * A traveler passing  
* Dialogue:  
  * (NPC) ”What are you doing out here?”  
  * (Player) “I am looking for some herbs.”  
  * (NPC) “Well that I can’t help you. But I have to warn you, if you’re staying out here for the night, don’t just pick the first place you see, that’s how you get into trouble.”  
  * (Player) “What should I look for?”  
  * (NPC) ““Ground, water, wind… and also what’s above you.”

**Scene 3: Evaluate Water Distance**

* Visual:  
  * River nearby  
  * Two possible spots: very close to water and slightly farther uphill  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…Water would help…But how close is too close?”  
* Interaction:  
  * Click near-water spot  
    * “…Too close and the ground is damp… and if it rains, this could flood.”  
  * Click farther spot  
    * “…Still close enough to reach.…Safer distance.”

**Scene 4: Evaluate Terrain and Ground**

* Visual:  
  * Three possible spots: Low dip area, Slight slope, Flat elevated ground  
* Dialogue:  
  * “These all look fine at first…But what happens if the weather changes?”  
* Interaction:  
  * Click Low dip area  
    * “…Water will collect here.Cold air too.”  
  * Click Slight slope  
    * “…Water will run off…But I won’t rest well.”  
  * Click Flat elevated ground  
    * “…Dry and stable… this place will be better than the other if it rains.”

**Scene 5: Evaluate Wind, Weather, and Exposure**

* Visual:  
  * Wind increasing slightly  
  * Two possible spots: Open area vs tree-covered area  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…Wind’s picking up…That will change things.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Click Open area  
    * “…Nothing to block the wind… Both starting fire and resting could be harder.”  
  * Click tree-covered area  
    * “…Less wind…But not without risk.”

**Scene 6: Scan Overhead and Surroundings**

* Visual:  
  * Two possible spots: Dead branches overhead vs Animal tracks on ground  
* Dialogue:  
  * “I have to check not just the ground… but also look up… and around…”  
* Interaction:  
  * Click branches  
    * “…Dead wood…If the wind gets stronger, this could fall.”  
  * Click animal track   
    * “…Animals pass through here.”

**Scene 7: Compare Campsites (Decision)**

* Visual:  
  * Two final campsite options shown side by side  
    * Site A: closer to water, lower ground, dense tree cover  
    * Site B: farther from water, flatter, more open, less tree  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…No place is perfect…I need to choose what matters more.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Select one campsite

**Scene 8: Final Safety Check**

* Visual:  
  * Selected campsite zoomed in  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…One more look. What happens if things get worse tonight?”  
* Interaction:（player can switch decision after final inspection）  
  * If Site A selected:  
    * Click water: “…Closer to water… that makes things easier now.”  
    * Click ground: “…But the ground is lower… if it rains, water could collect here.” “And the soil is damp… everything might stay wet through the night.”  
    * Click tree: “…And these trees…” “…If the wind gets stronger, those branches could fall.”  
    * Final Dialogue: “…This place works… but only if nothing changes too much.”  
  * If Site B selected:  
    * Click water: “…Farther from water… I’ll have to walk for it.”  
    * Click ground: “…But the ground is higher… water should drain away if it rains… better for resting and for fire.”  
    * Click tree: “…Nothing above me… no risk from falling branches.” “…But I’m exposed…If the wind picks up, I’ll feel it here.”  
    * Final Dialogue: “…Still… fewer things can go wrong if the weather turns.”  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…It’s not perfect…”  
  * “…But it will have to do.”  
  * “…I just need to be ready for what comes.”  
* Animation:  
  * Player places backpack at selected place  
* Interaction:  
  * Player take tent out of the backpack and set up the tent

**Scene 9: Discovering the Herbs**

* Visual:  
  * Campsite set up, player sitting in the camp looking outside  
  * Many bushes    
* Dialogue:  
  * “…That should hold for the night.”  
  * (glances at the bushes)  
  * “…Those leaves…That shape and color looks familiar…”  
* Animation:  
  * Move toward the bushes  
* Visual:  
  * Distinct herb features become noticeable  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…Wait…These match the ones in my notes…”  
  * “…Same structure… same edges…This is it.”  
* Interaction:  
  * Player click to collect the herb  
* Dialogue:  
  * “…So they were here all along…”  
  * “…This time… I can save those peoplet.”
